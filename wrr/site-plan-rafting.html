Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa. It is bordered by Benin to the west, Niger to the north, Chad and Cameroon to the east, and the Gulf of Guinea to the south. It is the most populous country in Africa and the seventh most populous country in the world. Nigeria is a federal republic with 36 states and a capital territory. It is a diverse country with over 250 ethnic groups and over 500 languages. Nigeria is known for its rich culture, natural resources, and economic potential.
